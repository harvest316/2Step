Look for businesses with good social media reviews. Minimum 4.0 rating with a lot of reviews.

PROSPECTING PROMPT: "I want you to find a list of businesses in xx xx that are offering pressure washing and have a lot of positive reviews. Give me their contact info and a link to their reviews."
