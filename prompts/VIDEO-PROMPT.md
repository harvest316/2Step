# Video Script Generator

You are creating a 30-45 second faceless video script for a local business. The video showcases a real customer review to make the business look amazing.

## Business Details

- **Business Name:** {{business_name}}
- **Category:** {{niche}}
- **City:** {{city}}
- **Review by:** {{review_author}}
- **Review text:** {{review_text}}

## Output Format

Generate a scene-by-scene video script in this exact format:

```
TITLE: [Business Name] — What Customers Are Saying

SCENE 1 (0-5s):
Visual: [describe background footage — relevant to the business niche]
Text overlay: "What customers are saying about [Business Name]..."
Music: Upbeat, modern, corporate-friendly

SCENE 2 (5-20s):
Visual: [describe footage that matches the review content]
Text overlay: "[Key quote from the review, broken into readable chunks]"
Voiceover: [Natural reading of the review excerpt]

SCENE 3 (20-30s):
Visual: [Complementary footage showing quality/professionalism]
Text overlay: "[Remaining review text or second powerful quote]"
Voiceover: [Continue review narration]

SCENE 4 (30-40s):
Visual: [Warm, inviting footage related to the business]
Text overlay: "⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — [Review Author]"
Voiceover: "Another happy customer of [Business Name] in [City]."

SCENE 5 (40-45s):
Visual: [Clean outro background]
Text overlay: "[Business Name] | [City] | Book Now"
Music: Fade out
```

## Guidelines

- Keep the video between 30-45 seconds
- Default aspect ratio: 9:16 (vertical — optimized for Reels/Shorts/TikTok)
- Use the actual review text — don't fabricate quotes
- Visual descriptions should be specific to the business niche (e.g., "clean modern kitchen" for a restaurant, "well-maintained lawn" for landscaping)
- Text overlays should be short (max 15 words per screen)
- Tone: Professional but warm, celebrating the business
- Do NOT include any pricing, phone numbers, or website URLs in the video
- The video is a free demo — it should make the business owner think "I need more of these"
